# 🌿 Health Pilot Design Principles

Version 1.0

---

## Purpose

Health Pilot exists to simplify health decisions.

People don't want more health data.

They want to know what to do next.

---

# Principle 1

## Increase computation.

## Reduce cognition.

Health Pilot should process more information,

so users can think less.

Complexity belongs inside the software,

not inside the user's head.

---

# Principle 2

## Action before explanation.

Always show what users should do first.

Explain why only when necessary.

---

# Principle 3

## Show less.

Every piece of information on the screen

must help today's decision.

If removing information improves the experience,

remove it.

---

# Principle 4

## One glance.

Users should understand today's priorities

within a few seconds.

Health Pilot should never require users

to analyze health data themselves.

---

# Principle 5

## AI exists to simplify.

AI should never overwhelm users.

The purpose of AI is not to provide more information.

The purpose of AI is to reduce uncertainty.

---

# Principle 6

## Calm.

Health should motivate,

not create anxiety.

Health Pilot guides.

It never judges.

---

# Final Principle

Every new feature should answer one question:

**Does this reduce the user's cognitive load?**

If the answer is no,

it does not belong in Health Pilot.
