# 🌿 Health Pilot Manifesto

Version 1.0

The first manifesto.

Written by the founder.

July 1, 2026

---

## We believe

Health should not be complicated.

Technology should reduce stress, not create it.

AI should guide people, not replace them.

---

# Our Mission

Turn health data into daily action,

for every human, without exception.

健康データを、今日の行動へ。

一人も例外のいない、すべての人のために。

---

# Our Promise

Health Pilot will always answer one question:

> "What should I do today?"

---

# Principles

## 1.

Simple before powerful.

Complexity belongs inside the software,
not inside the user's head.

---

## 2.

Action before information.

Health data is only valuable if it changes today's behavior.

---

## 3.

Privacy first.

Users own their health data.

Health Pilot never owns it.

---

## 4.

Built-in AI must always be free.

Everyone deserves health guidance.

---

## 5.

AI providers are replaceable.

Health Pilot belongs to users,
not to one AI company.

---

## 6.

Beautiful software is easier to use.

Every screen should feel calm.

---

## 7.

Less is better.

Show only what matters today.

---

## 8.

Health is a journey.

Progress matters more than perfection.

---

## 9.

The user is always in control.

AI recommends.

People decide.

---

## 10.

Build for everyone.

Start by helping one person.

---

## 11.

Health knowledge should be available to everyone.

Good health should never depend on money.

---

# Declaration

We believe that good health should never depend on

- wealth
- knowledge
- language
- where someone was born

Health Pilot exists

to help every human

make healthier decisions,

every day,

without exception.

---

一人も例外のいない、

すべての人が、

毎日ほんの少しでも

健康になれる世界を目指します。
