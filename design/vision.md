# Health Pilot Vision

## Mission

Turn health data into daily action.

健康データを、今日の行動へ。

---

## Vision

Health Pilot is a personal AI health copilot that helps people make better daily health decisions without requiring health expertise.

Health Pilot helps people make better decisions by understanding today's capacity, not by maximizing health metrics.

Users should never wonder,

"What should I do today?"

Health Pilot answers that question every day.

---

## Core User Concept

Today's Capacity is the primary concept shown to users.

Today's Capacity represents the AI-estimated amount of usable physical and mental resources available today.

It is estimated from:

- Health Signals
- Daily Context
- Calendar
- Weather
- Recent trends
- Historical patterns

Health Score remains an internal metric only.

---

## Philosophy

Apple Health stores health data.

Health Pilot transforms health data into action.

---

## AI Strategy

Health Pilot must never depend on one AI provider.

Supported providers:

- Built-in AI
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Ollama
- Future providers

The Built-in AI must always work for free.

External AI providers enhance the experience but are optional.

---

## Product Principles

Simple.

Fast.

Private.

Actionable.

Understand first.

Explain only when asked.
