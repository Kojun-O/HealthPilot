# Health Pilot Vision

## Mission

Turn health data into daily action.

健康データを、今日の行動へ。

---

## Vision

Health Pilot helps people become healthier without requiring health knowledge.

Users should never wonder,

"What should I do today?"

Health Pilot answers that question every day.

---

## Philosophy

Apple Health stores health data.

Health Pilot transforms health data into action.

---

## AI Strategy

Health Pilot must never depend on one AI provider.

Supported providers:

- Built-in AI
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Ollama
- Future providers

The Built-in AI must always work for free.

External AI providers enhance the experience but are optional.

---

## Product Principles

Simple.

Fast.

Private.

Actionable.

Beautiful.
